Low Carb Macadamia, Choco-Coconut Muesli

524 kcal
up to 15 minutes

Essential recipeingredients

2/3 tablespoon, whole pieces Pumpkin Seeds
1 squares Excellence Intense Dark Chocolate 85% Cocoa by Lindt
1/3 cup, whole pieces Macadamia Nuts
1/2 tablespoon Flaked Coconut Unsweetened
1/2 tablespoon Coconut, Dried, Shredded Or Flaked
1/3 tablespoon Coconut Oil
2/3 teaspoon Stevia liquid

Nutrients:

7 g of protein
53 g of fat
6 g of carbs
1 serving

1. Cut the macadamia nuts into thin slices and chop up the chocolate.

2. In a mixing bowl, add the nuts along with the chocolate, pumpkin seeds, coconut flakes and shredded coconut.

3. Melt the coconut oil and add into the mixture. Then sprinkle in the xylitol and stir everything together until the chocolate melts.

4. Let it cool before serving with ice cold almond milk.

  • So, what actually is the Keto Diet?

    The Ketogenic, or the keto diet, is a rich in fats low-carb diet with moderate protein intake. During the ketogenic diet, you get 75% of your calories from fat, 20% from protein, and only 5% of the total calorie intake comes from carbohydrates. Due to such a ratio, our body switches its preferred fuel source from carbohydrates to fat, which results in the production of an additional fuel source known as ketones. This unique metabolic condition is known as ketosis.

  • How can I tell if I am in ketosis?

    There are some signs that you are in ketosis: weight loss, increased urination; bad breath and a fruity, metallic taste in your mouth; mental sharpness; increased energy; sometimes insomnia. Blood and urine tests may show whether you are in ketosis. You can also use ketone urine testing strips to test for ketone bodies. Besides, you can test your blood using a keto meter.

  • What is a Keto-Flu?

    During the first days of keto diet, it is possible to experience some symptoms called Keto Flu. The Keto-flu is a feeling of mental fog, exhaustion, and possibly nausea due to keto-adaptation. Its a natural response of your body to switching from burning carbohydrates to burning fat as the main source of energy. A well-formulated Ketogenic Diet can often alleviate these symptoms. You may experience all of these symptoms, or just some of them. Dont worry, its temporary!
